Below is an unconfirmed transcript from Galactic Patrol of the Battle on Namek.

  • According to a transmission from a satellite not far from Namek, the battle began on December 18th of Age 762
  • On that day a couple of earthlings arrived on Namek, as well as a Saiyan who we believe was once the prince of thier bloodthirsty race
  • When the prince arrived he slaughtered a few of Frieza's soldiers.
  • Frieza responded by calling in the Legendary Ginyu Force
  • Another Saiyan, who apparently came up from Earth, decided to team up Frieza and they amazingly were able to defeat the Ginyu Force
  • Frieza and the earthlings were all looking for the Namekian dragon balls
  • Frieza killed many Namekians and almost all of the warriors that got in his way
  • The Namekian Dragon was called forth (The Satellite could not record what was wished for)
  • After the Prince was killed, the other saiyan fought Frieza and looked like he might have a chance
  • A blinding Golden Flash happened and the rest of our communications are lost
  • We find out later the planet has exploded
  • End Transmission.
