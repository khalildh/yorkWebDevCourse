The Beautiful Alberta Falls

Alberta Falls is one of the most spectacular waterfalls in Rocky Mountain National Park.

It was given its name in the 1800s by Abner Sprague and his wife, who were two of the first people to live in the park.

The waterfall can be reached by hiking just a half mile along a gentle uphill trail from the Glacier Gorge Trailhead.

There are many beautiful lookouts along the trail. Come check it out!
